Watering Schemes


Different plants have different needs. For this reason, two schemes have been developed for watering the plants, as described below.

Scheme I

Scheme I is the default option. It keeps the plants at a more constant moisture level by watering them in smaller increments, and more often. This is a the default option, and ideal for most plants.

Scheme II

Scheme II lets the plant dry out significantly before watering it. Some plants grow better under these conditions. Use this option if you know your plant prefers this type of watering cycle.
